Project Overview

Data: National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) 2021-2023 cycle

Exposure: Total Water Intake (g)

Outcomes: Type 2 Diabetes (doctor diagnosis, binary); HbA1c (%)

Covariates: Age, Sex, Race/Ethnicity, Education, Family Income-to-Poverty Ratio, BMI, Smoking history, Energy intake (kcal), Sugar intake (g)
